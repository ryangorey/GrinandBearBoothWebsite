How Long Is Studio Booth Open For?

We recommend a 3 hour experience for Studio Booth. Opening when the dance floor opens will have guests rushing to the booth to get their photos taken! Additional time can be added if needed.

Does Studio Booth Print Photos or Just Digitally Share?

Studio Booth has the ability to print onsite and utilizes digital sharing to deliver photos. Printing in 2×6” and 4×6” formats, guests get a printed copy and can share to their phones. At the end of the night, you'll receive an online gallery with all photos.

What Backdrops Are Included?

Our Stretch Fabric backdrops are included with Studio Booth. You can opt to upgrade to a Floral Wall design as well — view our backdrop options.

Are Props Included?

Yes, we offer physical props for our Studio Booth upon request. If you'd like a playful or themed touch, just let us know in advance and we'll bring a curated selection of props.
