How Does Social Booth Get Delivered?

Our team will deliver and setup Social Booth prior to the start of your event. The booth will be open for use throughout the full event. At the end, our team will return to the venue to pick the booth up.

Does Social Booth Print Photos or Just Digitally Share?

Social Booth utilizes digital sharing to deliver photos, GIFs, and Boomerangs. Since we don't print with this experience, the booth stays all night long with no attendant present. Guests share photos to their phones, and you'll receive an online gallery after the event.

What Backdrops Are Included?

Our Stretch Fabric backdrops are included with Social Booth. You can opt to upgrade to a Floral Wall design as well — view our backdrop options.

Are Props Included?

We provide a fun selection of digital props built right into the booth's software. Guests can choose from a variety of virtual accessories applied to their faces in real time using face-tracking technology.
